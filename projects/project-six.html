Bolverk XR – Cabin Crew Training in VR

Worked as graphical engineer on Bolverk XR, a VR training application for cabin crew, developed for real-world aviation scenarios. I was responsible graphical optimisation, shader development, and ensuring a smooth experience on Oculus Quest devices. The project involved close collaboration with designers and artists to create immersive environments while maintaining high performance standards.
