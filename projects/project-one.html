WORLDS FRVR

Browser based multiplayer game made in our in-house engine. Fully procedural World. Game focusing on User-Generated-Content and social features. I worked on a wide range of systems, from core gameplay systems to rendering and multiplayer systems, and led the game development as game lead.

Highlights
  • Procedural content generation for biomes, real-time growing trees, crafting systems including tooling for our artists.
  • Gameplay systems for multiplayer UGC systems, such as sharing systems and world customization.
  • Implemented animation systems, culling systems, and GPU particles from scratch in our in-house web engine.
  • led cross-disciplinary teams of around 10 people developing the game from concept through live service.
  • Product played a key role in a multi-million dollar deal with Meta.
